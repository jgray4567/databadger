HOW WE SOURCE YOUR DATA

Public Records, Enhanced Data

Every data point traces back to a public record. We organize it into three tiers so you always know exactly where your data comes from.

Recorded Data

Tier 1

Direct from government filings. County assessor records, court filings, recorder documents, and tax recordsexactly as filed, no interpretation.

  • Property ownership & transfers
  • Court case filings (probate, divorce, foreclosure)
  • Tax assessments & delinquencies
  • Liens, judgments & code violations

Derived Analytics

Tier 2

Calculated from recorded data using transparent formulas. Automated Valuations, equity estimates, LTV ratios, and our proprietary Distress Score™.

  • AVM (Automated Valuation Model)
  • Equity & LTV calculations
  • Distress Score™ (0-100 composite)
  • Days-on-market & price trends

Appended Contacts

Tier 3

Skip-traced from publicly available sources. Phone numbers, email addresses, mailing addressesso you can reach the right person immediately.

  • Phone numbers (mobile & landline)
  • Email addresses
  • Mailing & forwarding addresses
  • Relative & associate connections
